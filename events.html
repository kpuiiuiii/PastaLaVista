Private Events


Located in a 19th-century brownstone in Chicago’s historic Gold Coast neighborhood, Pasta La Vista offers a warm and inviting atmosphere for any celebration or gathering, a place where life’s greatest experiences unfold, and lifelong relationships and memories are created and celebrated.

We are honored you are considering having your event with us and are excited to work with you to create a custom event perfectly tailored to your needs. Our entire upstairs space is available for private events with the Dearborn Room and Vintage Room. The Dearborn Room, overlooking the charm and bustle of the historic neighborhood, seats 32 while the Vintage Room, boasting the building’s original detailed woodwork and built-ins housing Pasta La Vista’s extensive wine collection, seats 18. The entire space offers a feeling of warmth and intimacy for groups up to 50.

At Pasta La Vista, we embrace the spirit of Italy by using the best local ingredients simply, seasonably, and sincerely. Private dining menus change seasonally and vary from passed hors d’oeuvres to multi-course plated dinners. All event menus are tailored to the individual client and beverage packages are also available.

We charge no room fees but instead establish minimums to reserve the space. All events are subject to 20% service charge, 11.5% sales tax and 2% booking fee.

*Private Event Spaces are available for lunch, dinner and weekend brunch.

The Dearborn Room: Seats up to 32

The Dearborn Room, located on the second floor of the restaurant, seats 32 and overlooking the charm and bustle of the historic neighborhood. It provides a private and elegant atmosphere for all types of events including rehearsal dinners, birthdays and corporate gatherings.

The Vintage Room: Seats up to 18

The Vintage Room, located on the second floor of the restaurant, seats 18 and boasts the building’s original detailed woodwork and built-ins housing Pasta La Vista’s extensive wine collection. This room provides a more intimate atmosphere for smaller groups including business dinners, family gatherings and birthdays.

Entire Upstairs: Seats up to 50

This space includes the Dearborn Room and Vintage Room located on the second floor ash can accommodate up to 50. This space combines the warmth of the Dearborn Room with the intimacy of the Vintage Room for a truly memorable event.
